# Engaño Industrial: El Hombre en el Medio Modbus — Operational Technology | 200 pts

## Description

> A small simple industrial network, made up of an HMI (user interface) and two PLCs (Modbus/TCP servers), has been compromised.
>
> Security at the network layer has failed. An attacker has exploited the inherent trust in local networks by executing an ARP Poisoning (or Spoofing) attack.
>
> The goal of this attack was not to map the network. The attacker used ARP poisoning to redirect all traffic from the HMI and PLC through their own machine, positioning themselves as a Man-in-the-Middle (MITM).
>
> Once established as MITM, the attacker can not only observe data, but also has the ability to inject malicious Modbus commands.
>
> This is part 1/2 of the challenge. What is the attacker's IP? Flag format: `ikerlan{ip}`
