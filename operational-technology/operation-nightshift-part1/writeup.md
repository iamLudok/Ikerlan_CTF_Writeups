# Operation Nightshift - Industrial Sabotage Investigation (Part 1) — Operational Technology | 200 pts

## Description

> MediterráneoTech Industries, a water treatment plant in Mallorca, has suffered a cyberattack during the night shift on October 21, 2025.
>
> The plant uses an industrial monitoring system with an HMI server configured in READ-ONLY mode to visualise the process state. This server has no write permissions on the PLCs due to security policies.
>
> During the early hours, alarms went off: someone modified critical values in the PLC, writing dangerous setpoints that could have caused physical damage to the infrastructure. Additionally, the security team suspects that confidential information has been exfiltrated from the systems.
>
> The operations team captured the network traffic and now needs your help to investigate exactly what happened.
>
> This is part 1/3 of the challenge. Each part has its own flag.
>
> **Part 1 (Malware Hash):** What is the SHA256 hash of the implant used by the attacker? Flag format: `ikerlan{sha256_hash}`
