## Description

> The university traffic light has broken down and is only showing a strange sequence of lights. The technicians have managed to capture the digital signal, but they need your help to decode it.
>
> The captured sequence is:
> ```
> 01101001 01101011 01100101 01110010 01101100 01100001 01101110 01111011
> 01110010 01101111 01101010 01101111 01011111 01100001 01101101 01100001
> 01110010 01101001 01101100 01101100 01101111 01011111 01110110 01100101
> 01110010 01100100 01100101 01011111 01100010 01100101 01110000 01011111
> 01100010 01100101 01110000 01111101
> ```
>
> Can you find the hidden message?

## Hints

> **Hint 1 (free):** Binary numbers can represent ASCII characters. Each group of 8 bits (1 byte) represents one character.

> **Hint 2 (free):** You can use online binary-to-ASCII conversion tools, or a programmer's calculator. You can also do it manually: each bit has a value.
